[Tokens](./Token.md) the [harness](./Harness.md) sends on each [model provider request](./Model%20provider%20request.md). Billed at a lower rate than [output tokens](./Output%20tokens.md).

**中文：** [Harness](./Harness.md)在每次[模型提供商请求](./Model%20provider%20request.md)时发送的[词元](./Token.md)。计费费率低于[输出词元](./Output%20tokens.md)。

*Usage:*

"Bill's high but the [agent](./Agent.md)'s barely writing anything."

**中文：** "账单很高，但[智能体](./Agent.md)几乎没写什么内容。"

"It's the input tokens — every [turn](./Turn.md) re-sends the whole [session](./Session.md). Without the [prefix cache](./Prefix%20cache.md) you re-pay for the history each request."

**中文：** "是输入词元的问题——每个[轮次](./Turn.md)都会重新发送整个[会话](./Session.md)。没有[前缀缓存](./Prefix%20cache.md)的话，每次请求你都要为历史记录重新付费。"
